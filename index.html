📦 Stow & Go

Test your observation and decision-making skills!

  • Maximum score is 100.
  • Scoring: +10 points for each correct decision.
  • Time decreases every two videos: 60s → 50s → 40s → 30s → 20s.
  • Watch the Video Carefully – Review the video to determine if a stowing action took place.
    You may replay the video as many times as needed within the time limit.
  • Identify the Action – If stowing occurred, select "Stow" and choose the correct bin where the product was placed.
    If the action is anything other than stow, simply select the appropriate option — no bin selection is required.
  • Submit Before Time Runs Out – If you don’t answer within the given time, the game will automatically move to the next video.
  • Stay Focused – Accuracy and speed matter. Use your replays wisely and make your selection confidently before the timer ends.

📦 Stow

Select this option and the appropriate box when the item can be placed in a bin.

🚫 No Stow

Choose this when the item cannot be stowed due to a clear reason, like being oversized or damaged.

❓ Not Sure

Use this if you are uncertain whether the item can be stowed but a "Stow" or "No Stow" decision is possible with more inspection.

🤷 Unable to Determine

This is for when a decision is impossible to make due to a lack of information, such as the video being blurry or the item being completely out of view.
